Onym Words: A Vocab Game

Designed by Eric Duanmu, Angela Maliakal, Daniel Lee, and Brian Savage

Featuring the Merriam-Webster Collegiate Thesaurus and Dictionary APIs





How to Play


1. Two words will be shown. Your job is to decide whether the words are synonyms, antonyms, or neither.

2. Once you answer, you can hover over the words for their definition.

3. When you are ready, click NEXT to get your next pair of words.

4. You have 3 lives. The game ends when you run out!
